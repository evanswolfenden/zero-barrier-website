Making Metal 3D Printing Practical

Existing Metal 3D Printing technology today is extremely slow and expensive. 90% of installed printers today are multi-million dollar laser machines that can take days to print just one part. This severely limits the number of new ideas a company can prototype and prevents the added functionality of 3D printing from entering production. Zero Barrier has a patent pending printer that will change all of that. It prints 60x faster than existing printers, creating a 17x lower cost per part.

Right now we are upgrading our printer, testing the material strength, and working with select beta customers to verify our technology. We plan on opening up our services in November of this year.
